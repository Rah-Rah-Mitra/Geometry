In most differential geometry textbooks, only Riemannian connections are 
introduced on Riemannian manifolds. Non-metric connections are not even 
discussed. However, when considering families of probability distributions as 
manifolds, we find that the natural connections which one would introduce are 
non-metric (see §2.3). As we shall discuss in Chapter 3, this leads us to the 
novel notion of dual connections.
