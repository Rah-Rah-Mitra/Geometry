5.4. STABLE SYSTEMS AND STABLE FEEDBACK

We have not described the meaning of the geometric structure from the point 
of view of control theory. In this context also, however, it is again possible to 
introduce two potential functions and a divergence for which the Pythagorean 
theorem holds. This can be used in designing systems and feedback. For  
example, it can be helpful in designing feedback systems which best approximate the 
properties of some underlying system. This is a topic for future research.
