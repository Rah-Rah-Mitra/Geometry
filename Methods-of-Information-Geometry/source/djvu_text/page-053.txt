Finally, let us give a brief look at what happens when X is infinite. For a 
finite X, we have seen that V is an open set of the affine space A\ and therefore 
is a manifold with the tangent space Tp{P) isomorphic to A$ at each point p. 
Intuitively speaking, the essence of this fact is that the constraint p(x) > 0
