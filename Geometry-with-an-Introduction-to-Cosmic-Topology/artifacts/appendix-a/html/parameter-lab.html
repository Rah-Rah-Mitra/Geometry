Appendix A: List of Symbols: parameter lab

Turn the source symbol list into an executable glossary that links notation to the course models and helper functions.

Visual inspection targets

  • Symbol family map: Notation clusters by the geometry it supports.
  • Complex-plane symbols: z, conjugate(z), |z|, and arg(z) are one computational package.
  • Transformation symbols: Maps, groups, and invariants are tied together.
  • Curvature symbols: k, radius, circumference, and area formulas belong together.
  • Surface symbols: H_g, C_g, and chi organize the surface catalog.
  • Cosmic topology symbols: Omega terms and observable radii bridge geometry and data.
