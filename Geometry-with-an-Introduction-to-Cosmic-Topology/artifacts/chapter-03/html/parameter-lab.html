Transformations: parameter lab

Understand transformations by watching what they preserve: angles, clines, fixed points, cross ratios, and normal-form dynamics.

Visual inspection targets

  • Basic complex transformations: Translation, rotation, dilation, and affine maps move the same test grid differently.
  • Reflections compose to motions: Parallel mirrors give translations; intersecting mirrors give rotations.
  • Circle inversion: The product of distances to the inversion center is constant.
  • Clines map to clines: Lines and circles are one family under inversion.
  • Stereographic projection: Infinity appears as the north pole of a sphere model.
  • Mobius fixed-point dynamics: Normal forms move points along or around cline families.
